FAQ

Questions, clearly
answered.

Short, direct answers about how Roarly creates videos and how its plans work.

What does Roarly generate?+

Roarly creates short animated videos from text prompts, images, or both together.

Can I use an image and a prompt together?+

Yes. Use the image as visual direction and the prompt to describe the desired scene and movement.

How long can videos be?+

Video duration is a total-video limit, not a per-scene limit. Free supports up to 5 seconds, Starter up to 10 seconds, and Pro up to 30 seconds.

What are credits used for?+

Credits are used when you generate a video. The amount depends on output duration, resolution, and selected generation settings.

Why can generation take time?+

Generation runs through a queue and video processing takes time. Higher plans can include faster or priority queue access.

What happens if generation fails?+

The final credit-recovery policy will be published before checkout launches. Failed jobs must not silently consume credits.

Can I use videos commercially?+

Commercial use is included in the Pro and Business plan direction. Final rights will be defined in the published terms.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. Subscription management and cancellation will be available in the connected account billing portal.
